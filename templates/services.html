WHY US?

WHY CHOOSE DAN SCHOOL?

As the world’s leading premium international schools organisation, we make every moment of your child’s education count. Our strong academic foundations combine world-class teaching and curricula with cutting-edge technology and facilities, to create learning experiences like no other. Inside and outside of the classroom, we inspire our students to achieve more than they ever thought possible.

No two children learn the same way, which is why a Nord Anglia education personalises learning to what works best for your child. Our approach means that your child leaves school with everything they need to succeed in whatever they choose to be or do in life.

Chief Executive Officer

×

Andrew Fitzmaurice

Following Andrew’s appointment as CEO in 2003, Nord Anglia has established itself as the world’s leading premium schools organisation.

Under Andrew’s leadership, Nord Anglia provides personalised learning to thousands of students aged 2–18 years old in premium international day and boarding schools. Nord Anglia’s global family of schools are located in more than 30 countries across Asia, the Americas, the Middle East and Europe. Andrew graduated from the University of Wales Cardiff, Magna Cum Laude, with a degree in Economics. He also holds a master's degree in International Education from King’s College London, one of the world’s leading universities. Earlier in his career, Andrew held a number of senior leadership positions including Chief Executive Officer of easyCar.com, an international online automobile rental company. He also spent 13 years at TNT Express UK as a member of its senior leadership team.

Ton van Vilsteren

He has more than two decades of experience in the hospitality sector, including senior finance leadership roles across Asia Pacific, Europe, the Middle East and Latin America. Ton previously led the financial direction of hotels, commercial real estate, retail operations and education at the Jumeirah Group based in Dubai. George Ghantous

He started his working career in 1993 as an engineer in the oil and gas industry, followed by a successful career in the construction industry. Dr Elise Ecoff

Chief Education Officer

Dr Elise Ecoff is the Chief Education Officer of Nord Anglia Education.

×

Dr Elise Ecoff

Dr Elise Ecoff is Nord Anglia Education's Chief Education Officer. Under her leadership, the Education Team ensure that our schools' educational practice, curricula and programmes are second to none. Comprised of senior education experts, the team drives a research-led approach to educational excellence. In addition to providing specialist support to our principals and teachers, they lead best practice and continuous improvement programmes informed by the latest thinking in teaching and learning, ensuring our schools offer the finest educational experience.

Prior to taking up this role in 2020, Elise was Principal at North Broward Preparatory School from 2012 where she had served since 2004 as a division leader, curriculum specialist, and Assistant Head of School. She also spent fifteen years working in Broward County Public Schools in Florida.

Serving more than 34 years in education in teaching and leadership roles has enabled Elise to learn much from students, families, teachers, and education experts. Her time leading schools fulfilled a lifelong dream and taught her more than she expected about the power of education, and the many and various ways learning empowers.

Elise is also committed to helping schools on their continuous improvement journey and as such spent four years as a member of the board of directors of the Florida Council of Independent Schools.

As the founder of MNT2LEAD, a website designed to serve as a ‘digital mentor,’ Elise strives to build community and pathways for aspiring female educational leaders.

Elise holds a doctorate in Educational Leadership from Lynn University, a master’s degree in Educational Leadership from Nova Southeastern University and a bachelor’s degree in Elementary Education, with a minor in Special Education and certification in ESOL, from Florida International University.

Vanessa Nicholls

Vanessa joined Nord Anglia in January 2023 in the newly created role of Group Strategic Programmes Director. She provides executive leadership for both the group’s edtech programme and its global alumni programme.

Prior to her 25-year career in the UK Civil Service, Vanessa worked for the National Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Children as well as within a secondary school in London. Her Civil Service Career began in the Department for Education where she worked on programmes to support parents, children in care, and access to Higher Education. She has held various senior leadership positions in government, overseeing the delivery of major programmes on improving opportunities for young people, international partnerships, and organisational performance.

Vanessa cares deeply about encouraging leadership development, creating environments where people flourish, and supporting younger women to develop their careers.

Scott Kelly

Scott Kelly

Scott Kelly joined Nord Anglia Education as the Chief Human Resources Officer in January 2022.

He has nearly 30 years of international experience in strategic HR, with a strong track record in growing and developing talent, culture building, and creating engagement across global organisations.

Scott previously worked for Telenav, a global software company, and formerly held executive HR and operational leadership roles at Hitachi Vantara and Hitachi Data Systems.

One of Scott’s many achievements at Hitachi Data Systems was helping the company be recognised by Fortune magazine as one of the 100 Best Companies to Work For and one of its 100 Best Workplaces for Millennials.

Scott holds a post graduate degree in Human Resource Management from the University of Strathclyde, a bachelor’s in biology/biotechnology from the University of Paisley, and has taken part in various leadership courses from prestigious institutions including Dartmouth’s School of Business and the IMD Business School Lausanne, Switzerland.

WHY US?

WORKING FOR DAN SCHOOL?

Outstanding teachers make outstanding schools, which is why our educators are the best in the world. Our teachers never stop learning through our world-class professional development programme, including opportunities with MIT, King’s College London and Juilliard.

We combine a wealth of experience with a passion for education – and nowhere is this more evident than in our leadership team.

Our regional leaders bring a wealth of experience to the management of Nord Anglia schools, no matter where they are around the world.

The Education Advisory Board provides valuable external perspectives to develop our offering. Working closely with Nord Anglia’s leadership team, they support the continuous development of programmes, curricula, teaching and learning to deliver outstanding academic, social and personal outcomes for students.

Our global family of premium international day and boarding schools

With across the globe and tens of thousands of students worldwide, we are a truly global community that spans both continents and cultures. By joining us as a student and parent, you become part of our international family.
