A SUPPORTIVE ENVIRONMENT BUILT FOR SUCCESS

The British International School of Washington is committed to providing a safe, happy, student-centered learning environment where students are inspired to succeed. Our campus is conveniently close to the centre of Washington and next door to Dumbarton Oaks Park, the Naval Observatory, and the National Cathedral. We take full advantage of our fantastic location and also enjoy the many world-class museums at our doorstep.

Our campus is fun and welcoming! Our students love that we’re all housed in one building – this helps us to develop a true sense of school spirit across all the ages, interests, and cultural backgrounds we have in our community.

OUR SCHOOL FACILITIES

OUR SCHOOL FACILITIES

At the British International School of Washington, we have a unique one-building system that places all year groups under one roof. As a result, we have a close-knit community and highly integrated learning environment.

Our welcoming facilities place your child in state-of-the-art, balanced spaces that brings their learning to life. With cutting-edge equipment and seamless interactive technology in your child’s classroom, your child will grow both in and out of the classroom.

BIS Washington campus features

  • Advanced science labs
  • Music suite with a recording studio
  • Performing arts theatre and an art studio
  • International Baccalaureate (IB) lounge
  • Library for our Primary School students
  • Outdoor playgrounds and an indoor gym
  • Dance studio

INTEGRATING SMART TECHNOLOGY

TECH FOR PURPOSEFUL LEARNING

In our teacher-led, tech-enabled classrooms, your child will have all the tech they need at their fingertips for purposeful learning.

Whether they're in STEAM classes in Primary School, computer science modules in Secondary School, or special elective classes, our students feel empowered to use a variety of tools, including ones with all the digital skills they need to thrive in our world.

In fact, we’re excited to open our newly renovated, state-of-the-art Makerspace from the 2022–23 school year, where our students will continue to create incredible projects for years to come.
