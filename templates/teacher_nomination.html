Admission Entry RequirmentsTeacher Evaluation Nomination Form

Applicants for primary school must submit one evaluation from:

  • A classroom or homeroom teacher from the current or most-recent school.

Applicants for secondary school must submit evaluations from:

  • An English teacher from the current or most-recent school,
  • A Maths teacher from the current or most-recent school,
  • And a secondary school leader, such as a current school counselor, administrator, or other school leader who has regular interactions with the student.

To the parents/guardians:

Please use this form to nominate referees for the confidential teacher/school leader evaluations for your child's admission application. Referees will be sent the evaluation form directly by the school upon your submission of this form.

You will need to submit this form for each child applying to the British International School of Washington.
