CONTACT US

The Dan School of Abuja is located on the corner of Wisconsin Avenue and Whitehaven Street, NW. Our entrance is on the courtyard side of the building, off Whitehaven Street. The doors on the Wisconsin Avenue side of the building are emergency exits only.

DIRECTIONS

From the Beltway (I-495) North

From I-495, take at the Wisconsin Ave Exit (Exit 34). Head South towards Georgetown for approximately 6 miles. Pass the National Cathedral on your left, cross Massachusetts Ave, continue for 1 mile. Turn left on Whitehaven Street, just past the Trader Joe's on your left.

From the Key Bridge

After crossing the Key Bridge into Washington, turn right turn onto M Street. Turn left at the second traffic light onto 33rd Street. Follow 33rd Street to the top of the hill and turn left onto Wisconsin Ave heading North, continue for .3 miles. Turn right on Whitehaven Street, just past the Safeway Grocery Store on your right.

From McLean

Take Chain Bridge Road (VA-123N) towards Washington. Turn Left on Glebe Road and cross the Potomac. Turn Right on Canal Road. Turn left on Arizona Ave, Nw. At the first light, turn right on MacArthur Blvd, NW. Continue for 2.6 miles (MacArthur Blvd becomes Reservoir Road, NW). Turn left onto Wisconsin Ave heading North, continue for .3 miles. Turn right on Whitehaven Street, just past the Safeway Grocery on your right.
